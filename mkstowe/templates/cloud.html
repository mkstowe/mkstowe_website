Project Cloud

Project Cloud is an FPS Action-Adventure game made in Unity by WolverineSoft Studio within the Unviersity of Michigan. Immersed in a world inspired by Greek mythology, the player takes on the role of Hermes and must explore the Cloud Interior - a giant cave-like system in the sky - to discover why clouds have stopped producing rain. Along the way, Hermes must fight unique creatures and solve the problem at hand in order to bring much needed rain back to the people of Earth.
